WHAT RDA IS ABOUT?
ABOUT

Radio-Imaging Diagnosis Accompaniment (RDA) will be a web-based platform for leveraging the radiology department digital potential within healthcare institutions, through integration of AI technologies, annotating functionality, report/findings sharing, expertise sharing, patient health records access, all through providing accompaniment throughout the integration process

MISSION

RDA aims to boost medical imaging analysis, exchange expertise and implement emerging artificial intelligence technologies to improve diagnosis, education and research, particularly in Morocco

VISION

RDA aims to become the first choice, worldwide, for leveraging medical imaging analysis and diagnosis through its easy to use automated functionalities and customizable computer-aided diagnosis

It is known that radiologists make mistakes when reading studies which lead to misdiagnosis. AI emergence aims to help radiologists with their diagnosis. One of the limitations is the scarce of available datasets for public use. Even though, number of datasets was very limited, AI applications on medical data showed how much potential they hold. Medical imaging, in particular radiology, has been getting a fair amount of interest from the AI community these latest years, and this field will be the focus of our platform. So, our platform aims to boost medical imaging analysis with AI, exchange expertise and implement emerging artificial intelligence technologies to improve diagnosis, education and research, mainly in Morocco and some countries of Europe.

OUR VALUES
Accompaniment

RDA will help and work closely with medical/IT staff to shorten the gap between them and implementing emerging AI technologies

Knowledge Sharing

Worldwide experts can assess/review other reports/findings, between local public hospitals network in particular

Innovation

RDA will focus on giving the local radiologists and healthcare IT departments the power to benefit from a customizable use of machine learning technologies crossing the known limiting barriers (Federated learning..)

Accessibility

Open for university students
Patients can access their health care records

Transparency

Test, accept, validate and deploy open source AI projects
